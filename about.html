Our Story

Crafting Comfort,
Building Homes

Desian Vision Furniture was founded with a simple but powerful belief: every Kenyan home deserves beautiful, quality furniture that doesn't cost a fortune.

Based in Githurai 45, Nairobi, we specialise in custom-made furniture — sofas, beds, dining sets, wardrobes, and more. Every piece is handcrafted to order, using premium materials selected for quality and durability.

Our skilled artisans work closely with each client to understand their vision, taste, and space. We don't sell off-the-shelf — we create pieces that are uniquely yours.
